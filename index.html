Who We Are
About Ahsmir Ventures

Ahsmir Ventures is a registered general merchandise and logistics enterprise, certified by the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria. Founded in January 2020 and headquartered in Kaduna State, we have grown into a trusted name in Nigeria's agribusiness landscape.

We specialize in the sourcing, supply, marketing, and distribution of premium agricultural commodities and fertilizers, supported by a comprehensive end-to-end logistics infrastructure. Our deep roots in northern Nigeria's agricultural belt give us unmatched access to quality produce — from staple grains to specialty superfoods and premium spices.

We serve a diverse clientele including food processors, exporters, government agencies, NGOs, and institutional buyers, delivering consistently on quality, timeliness, and transparency.
